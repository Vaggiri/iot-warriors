Hardware Components ⚙️
ESP32 Development Board: The main microcontroller for the project.

DHT11 Sensor: Measures temperature and humidity.

MQ-7 Sensor Module: Detects carbon monoxide levels.

Neo6M GPS Module: Provides location data.

3.7V Lithium-Ion Battery: A rechargeable power source (e.g., 18650 cell).

TP4056 Charging Module: Manages the charging and discharging of the Li-ion battery and provides a stable output.

Breadboard: Used for assembling the circuit without soldering.

Jumper Wires: For making connections between all the components.
